Harmony

You rush to your dear old gnome friend Gourmando to talk and discover unsettling evidence that for years the villagers have been exploiting the land's resources. They took the sea's bounty for granted while polluting its waters.

Gourmando provides you with a magical recipe book and instructs you on the ingredients you need to gather in order to create dish powerful enough to enhance Bob's power before the next attack from The Devourer.
